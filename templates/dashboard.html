~~~Welcome to Wanderers Quill Dashboard~~~

The buttons on the side bar to the left of the page will direct you to different feature that our world building websit has for now. The "New Article" button give you access to the 6 article templates that we have for creating. The under it button called "Brower" gives you access to all existing articles sorted by category, provides a single point of contact for the user to browse all articles that they have created. The next one down is the "Community" button which allows for access to other users articles and see what kind of world builds they have made to either get inspirtaion from other users or to also use there world build for there own personal use.
